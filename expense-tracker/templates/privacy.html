Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter when logging expenses.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the service, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically, this includes:

  • Displaying your expense history and spending summaries.
  • Maintaining and securing your account.
  • Diagnosing technical issues and improving app performance.

We will never use your personal or financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate the platform — for example, hosting infrastructure. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only for the purposes of providing their service to us. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
